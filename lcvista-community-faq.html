LCvista Community FAQ

This is a living document that will be updated as new questions arise. Have a question that's not answered here? Please don't hesitate to reach out to community@lcvista.com with any questions, ideas, or feedback. We look forward to hearing from you!

I'm new, where do I begin?

It's great to see you here!

There's no right or wrong way to get started. Here are some ways to kick off your LCvista Community journey:

  • Make sure to update your profile so people can learn about you and find you and be sure to set up your site update notifications at your desired interval.
  • Check out the Groups tab for topic specific groups or the Forum (when a topic does not exist) for questions and answers that you might find helpful
  • Search for and reach out to peers under the People tab

Or simply explore on your own and reach out to us at community@lcvista.com if you have questions or feedback.

Are there any fees associated with this community?

Membership is free and open to all LCvista admins.

What's the mission of this community?

Our mission with the online LCvista Community is to provide a platform for LCvista firm site administrators to informally gather on demand for discussions with peer accounting industry learning and CPE compliance professionals to share knowledge, best practices, and leverage peer networking opportunities.

Are there guidelines for participating in discussions?

Be sure to follow our Code of Conduct when posting. Be kind and respectful of members at all knowledge levels.

If I see any issues or violations, how do I report them?

If you ever encounter a post or comment that violates these guidelines, please click on the three dots at the top right of the post and click "Report This Content." After you report content, our Community Team will review the post and take the appropriate action.

How can I network?

There are many ways to network in the LCvista community:

  • Respond to, or ask, questions in the Forum
  • Engage with posts in the Live Feed
  • Engage with members of the Groups you're a part of
  • Use Share your location in the top right menu to share with the community that you're attending an event or conference. It's a great way for others attending the same event to engage with you within the community or even in person
  • Search for other LCvista admins in the directory under the People tab. Select a profile and click on Send a message on the profile to send a direct message. Please make sure to follow the Code of Conduct when doing so
How do I make a post?

There is one primary way to post in our community: create a post in the topic specific Groups. When a topic does not exist you can use the Forum. Ask questions, find answers, and talk shop with other learning and compliance professionals. Search prior discussions to find other professionals with knowledge or similar interests, follow and contribute on open discussion threads, and create a new discussion thread seeking professionals with experience in specific areas of interest.

How do I ask for help with using the product?

The fastest way to get support is to reach out to support@lcvista.com. If you'd like to learn best practices at other firms on how they use a specific feature or workflow in LCvista, you can post a question in the topic specific groups or the Forum (when a topic does not exist).

I want to request a product feature or enhancement. Can I do that in the community?

All support, feature, or enhancement requests should continue to be directed to support@lcvista.com.

I want to ask a question in the community. What should I do?

A great place to start is to use the Search on the top right. Enter some keywords and see if the results list relevant content. If you don't find what you're looking for, head to the topic specific groups or the Forum (when a topic does not exist) to post your question. Can I promote my own services?

Posts that explicitly promote a product or service will be removed. Please refer to our Code of Conduct for more details.

How can I find resources?

We recommend checking out our Resources tab.

How can I give feedback on this community?

To make the LCvista Community a better space, we always look for ways to improve!

Please don't hesitate to reach out to community@lcvista.com with any questions, ideas, or feedback. We look forward to hearing from you!
